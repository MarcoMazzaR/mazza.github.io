About Me

My name is Marco Mazza, I'm 22 years old and I live in Venado Tuerto, Santa Fe, Argentina. I've finished my school studies in 2018 and discovered the enormous data world about 2 years ago.

My formation

My first steps in data were on a online academy from my country called "Coderhouse", taking the Data Analysis course that taught me intermediate/advanced skills on SQL and PowerBI. Then, I realized that was time for something more. I started to honing my skills in SQL and mainly in PowerBI, and decided to take my knowledge one step further by completing the Google's Data Analytics Certificate.
